Built for the way
real holidays work

Forward & forget
Forward any booking confirmation to your personal allbooked address. The AI reads it and adds the step automatically — flights, hotels, transfers, everything.

Scan in seconds
Take a photo of any confirmation email or document. AI extracts every detail — reference number, dates, times, airports — and fills the form for you.
📅
Day-by-day itinerary
See your entire trip laid out day by day. Multi-night hotels span every night. Export to Apple Calendar, Google Calendar or Outlook with one tap.

Smart packing lists
Pre-built packing lists you can customise for every trip. Tick off as you pack. Never leave your phone charger behind again.

Multi-currency finances
Track what you've paid and what's outstanding across any currency. Live exchange rates keep everything accurate. Payment reminders before due dates.

Travel memories
After your trip, add highlights — a great meal, a moment, a view. Past holidays become a beautiful travel journal you'll actually want to look back at.
