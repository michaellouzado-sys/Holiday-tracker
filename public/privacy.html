Important Disclaimer

allbooked is a planning and organisational aid only. It does not make bookings on your behalf. Always verify all travel details directly with your airline, hotel, or other travel providers before travelling. The accuracy of information depends entirely on what is entered or imported by the user. We accept no responsibility or liability for missed flights, cancelled bookings, incorrect information, or any other loss or inconvenience arising from use of this app.

allbooked is a holiday organiser app operated by Michael Louzado. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

1. What data we collect

Account data

  • Email address
  • Name (for your forwarding address)

Holiday data

  • Holiday names, destinations, dates
  • Booking details, packing lists, memories

Email forwarding

  • Forwarded emails are passed to Claude (Anthropic AI) for extraction
  • Raw emails are not stored permanently

Photo scanning

  • Images sent to Claude for extraction are never stored

2. How we use your data

  • To provide the allbooked service
  • To process email forwarding and photo scanning
  • To send authentication emails
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising

3. Where your data is stored

Data is stored on Supabase (EU servers). Email is processed by Resend. AI extraction by Anthropic — not used for model training per their API terms.

4. Data retention

  • Data retained while your account is active
  • Account deletion removes all data within 30 days
  • Photos are never stored. Forwarded emails deleted after extraction

5. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete or export your data. Contact: privacy@allbooked.app

6. Limitation of Liability

allbooked is provided as a planning tool only. We make no warranties regarding the accuracy or completeness of information in the app. We shall not be liable for any loss arising from use of the app including but not limited to missed travel, incorrect bookings, or financial loss. You remain solely responsible for verifying all travel arrangements with providers.

7. Cookies

Essential authentication cookies only. No tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Children

Not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
