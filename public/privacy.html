Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

allbooked ("we", "us", "our") is a holiday organiser app built and operated by Michael Louzado. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

1. What data we collect

Account data

  • Email address (used to create and identify your account)
  • Name (used to generate your personal email forwarding address)

Holiday data

  • Holiday names, destinations and dates you enter
  • Booking details you add (references, providers, dates, prices)
  • Packing lists and memories you create

Email forwarding data

  • When you forward a booking confirmation to your allbooked address, we receive the email content
  • We pass the email body to Claude (Anthropic's AI) to extract booking details
  • The extracted details are saved to your holiday. The raw email is not stored permanently

Photo scanning data

  • When you scan a confirmation photo, the image is sent to Claude (Anthropic) for extraction
  • Images are not stored — they are processed and discarded immediately

2. How we use your data

  • To provide the allbooked service — storing and displaying your holiday information
  • To process email forwarding and photo scanning via AI extraction
  • To send authentication emails (sign-up confirmation, password reset)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

3. Where your data is stored

Your account and holiday data is stored on Supabase, a cloud database provider, on servers in the EU. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth.

Email forwarding is processed by Resend, an email infrastructure provider.

AI extraction (photo scanning and email parsing) is performed by Anthropic (Claude API). Data sent to Anthropic is used only to process your request and is not used to train AI models under their API terms.

4. Data retention

  • Your account and holiday data is retained for as long as you have an account
  • If you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Forwarded emails are not stored after extraction is complete
  • Scanned photos are not stored at all

5. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@allbooked.app.

6. Cookies

allbooked uses only essential cookies required for authentication (session management). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Children

allbooked is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests: privacy@allbooked.app
